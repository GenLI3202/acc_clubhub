The **RAD RACE ONE TWENTY 2026** is a unique cycling festival scheduled for June 12–14, 2026, spanning the regions of Oberallgäu, Germany, and Bregenzerwald, Austria. The event’s main highlight is the **120km course on fully closed roads**, and it is strictly a **team-based format** (minimum 3 riders per team), catering to both high-speed racers and those riding for pure enjoyment. In addition to Sunday's main race, the weekend features a team time trial at Jochpass, a brand expo, and various social rides, making it a comprehensive celebration of cycling and team spirit.
