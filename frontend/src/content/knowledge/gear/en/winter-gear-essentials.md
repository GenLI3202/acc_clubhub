Winter cycling requires special gear to handle low temperatures and slippery roads.

## Essential Checklist

1. **Insulated Gloves** - Gore-Tex waterproof material
2. **Wind-proof Face Mask** - Protect from frostbite
3. **Winter Shoe Covers** - Keep feet warm
4. **Reflective Vest** - Low visibility in winter, safety first
