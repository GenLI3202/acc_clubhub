## Cleat System Comparison

| System | Features | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SPD-SL | Large contact area, efficient power transfer | Road cycling |
| SPD | Dual-sided, easy clip-in | Commuting/MTB |
| Look | Large float angle | Road cycling |
