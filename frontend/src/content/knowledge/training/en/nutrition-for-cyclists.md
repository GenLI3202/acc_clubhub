Proper nutrition is key to sustained cycling. This guide covers fueling strategies for all phases.

## During-Ride Fueling

- **Energy**: 60-90g carbohydrates per hour
- **Hydration**: 500-750ml per hour
- **Electrolytes**: Sodium and potassium essential for long rides
