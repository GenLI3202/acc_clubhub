Race day performance depends on prior preparation. This guide provides a complete checklist.

## Race Week

- Reduce volume, maintain intensity
- Get adequate sleep
- Check equipment

## Race Day

- Eat breakfast 2-3 hours before
- Warm up 20-30 minutes
- Stay calm, enjoy the race
