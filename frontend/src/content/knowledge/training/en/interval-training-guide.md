Interval training is one of the most effective ways to improve cycling performance.

## Classic Interval Workouts

- **30/30**: 30s all-out + 30s recovery, repeat 10-20 times
- **Tabata**: 20s max effort + 10s rest, repeat 8 times
- **Over-Under**: Alternating above and below threshold
