Cadence is a key factor in cycling efficiency. Different scenarios require different cadence strategies.

## Cadence Guide

| Scenario | Recommended | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Flat cruising | 85-95rpm | Optimal efficiency |
| Climbing | 70-85rpm | Protect knees |
| Sprinting | 100-120rpm | Maximum power output |
